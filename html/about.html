About Us


CodeBliss Learning is an online coding bootcamp dedicated to providing high-quality education in software development.
Our mission is to empower individuals to become proficient developers and excel in their careers.

-- Mission Statement and Values --

Our mission at CodeBliss Learning is to make coding education accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We value:

Inclusivity:
Providing an inclusive learning environment where everyone feels welcome.
Excellence:
Striving for excellence in teaching and learning.
Community:
Fostering a supportive community of learners and mentors.
Innovation:
Embracing innovation in curriculum design and teaching methodologies.
